About LifeGraph

LifeGraph combines Neo4j knowledge graphs and AI agents to help people navigate complex social services with confidence.

Instead of searching for programs one by one, LifeGraph helps you:

✓ Find What You Qualify For

Discover programs that match your unique situation.

✓ Follow the Best Path

See the most effective application sequence when programs unlock other opportunities.

✓ Get Connected to Help

Locate organizations, services, and contact information near you.

How It Works

Three specialized AI agents work together:

Eligibility Navigator

Answers: “What programs can I get?”

Pathway Advisor

Answers: “What's my path to stability?”

Resource Locator

Answers: “Where can I find help?”
